Tonik Tap Show

Lighting Design

Tonik, a tap dance group on campus, wanted their Spring 2017 show lit. The show was in a “black box”, basically an empty building called Shanley Pavilion. I had previously been assistant lighting designer for a show in Shanley.

Contribution

As head lighting designer, I planned, set up, and programmed the lights.

Links

Full Videos of the Show

Design

I designed and sketched the lighting configuration with my assistant lighting designer. After determining which lights to use, we ensured they were within our budget and ordered them.

Setup

We hung back lights, front lights, side lights, and foot lights in the space.

Then we focused all of the lights by turning them on and positioning the light beam where we wanted it.

Program

The last step is queuing, where we program the light configurarions into a queue which we step through during the show. We had 156 queues for this show.
