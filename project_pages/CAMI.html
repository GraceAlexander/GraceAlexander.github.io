CAMI Visualization Lab

UI Design

The CAMI Molecular Imaging Lab has a 3D wall on which researchers can view molecular images and other biological specimens.

Contribution

I designed the interaction and built the front end to allow users to interact with their 3D image on the wall.

Links

https://github.com/omega-hub/cami

Interface

The cube on the iPad screen maps to the molecule on the 3D screen. You can use one finger to rotate the cube, two fingers to move the cube around, and pinch movements with two fingers to zoom in and out. These movements manipulate the cube on the screen and the molecule on the wall

You can load a molecule with the left side bar and change the settings and learn more about how to use the screen on the right sidebar.
