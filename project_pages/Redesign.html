Assignments

I updated the assignments that teach Graphic and Interface Design. The previous assignments had just involved finding a website and discussing how the Graphic Design and Norman Design principles, respectively, related.

For the Graphic Design assignment, it was difficult for TA's to grade such different websites, so I created a bad graphic design and the assignment was to fix the design and discuss the changes made. The bad design is the first image, and the second image is an example fix.

Section Materials

I developed codepens with interactive web development tutorials. These are meant to be used during the discussion TA's teach.

They both have demo sections and then a section with activities for the students. The first introduces javascript and then the second introduces how to use frameworks, specifically JQuery, Bootstrap, and Material Design.
