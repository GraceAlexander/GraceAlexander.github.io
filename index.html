About

I am pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science and the Segal Design Certificate and will be graduating in March 2018.

I am from rural Virginia and love being outside, especially playing soccer and hiking. Some things that get me really excited are life hacks, upcycling, and making cold brew coffee.

I enjoy coding and building with other people and believe the best products come out of collaboration. I strive to continuously learn and explore new interests with side projects.

After school, I hope to use my experience in product design, interface design, and machine learning to build novel products that make people love technology.
